\section{Paper Identification}

This erratum refers to AAAI 2022 paper \emph{Goal Recognition as Reinforcement Learning}. \\
DOI: 10.1609/aaai.v36i9.21198\footnote{\url{http://doi.org/10.1609/aaai.v36i9.21198}}.

\section{Erratum Description}

Equation (5) in the original paper should be of the KL-divergence between the observed policy and the goal policy, rather than the other way around, and should read, as follows:

\setcounter{equation}{4}

\begin{equation}
    \begin{split}
        KL(\observation, Q_g) & = \kldiv(\pi_\observation \mid \mid \pi_g) = \\
        & \sum\limits_{i \in |\observation|} \pi_\observation(a_i \mid s_i) \log \frac{\pi_\observation(a_i \mid s_i)}{\pi_g(a_i \mid s_i)}
    \end{split}
\end{equation}
